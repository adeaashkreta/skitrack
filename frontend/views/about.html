About Ski Track

Our mission is to bring real-time snow and slope updates right to your fingertips.

Who We Are

Ski Track is more than just a snow report app—it's your ultimate mountain companion. Built by a team of passionate skiers, snowboarders, and developers, our mission is to redefine how winter sports enthusiasts access information. Frustrated by outdated and fragmented data, we created Ski Track to be a modern, comprehensive platform that delivers everything you need to plan your trip and make the most of your time on the mountain.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide accurate, real-time information across a wide network of ski resorts. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned pro, Ski Track helps you make smarter decisions with up-to-the-minute snow depth, live slope status, lift availability, and 7-day weather forecasts—all delivered through a fast, intuitive interface.

Our Vision

We envision a global hub for the winter sports community—where anyone, anywhere, can explore and compare resorts, track snow conditions, and discover activities tailored to their skills and interests. By centralizing data from hundreds of resorts, we aim to empower adventurers with transparency, choice, and excitement.

What We Offer

Ski Track gives you access to a growing list of resorts, each with detailed profiles that include trail maps, resort amenities, and operational updates. Our platform also features booking tools for ski and snowboard lessons, rental services, and upcoming mountain events—so you're always in the loop.

Unforgettable Experiences

From thrilling snowboarding sessions to guided ski tours and family-friendly festivals, Ski Track connects you with unforgettable alpine experiences. Whether you're carving through fresh powder or enjoying après-ski by the fire, we're here to help you make every moment count.
